Event proposal

Propose an event.

Any member can propose an event for their chapter. Your proposal opens a 5-day consent round — if no member raises a valid objection, it goes on the calendar automatically.

How the consent round works

Once submitted, all members of your chapter are notified by email. They have 5 days to consent or raise an objection. An objection isn't a veto — it's an invitation to resolve a specific concern. If the window closes with no objections, your event is approved and published.
